Migrating to Wintersmith

I’ve migrated my site from Octopress to Wintersmith. Seeing as I’m using Node.js a lot these days, I figured it was worth trying out a static site generator in the same language.

It didn’t take very long to get up and running. Customising the theme/template to my liking on the other hand took a while. The combination of Jade templating and CoffeeScript is fine for me but it may be a little off-putting if you’re not already familar with either.
